French Toast

Prep

5 min

Cook

15 min

Total

20 min

Servings

4

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 8 slices of bread
  • Butter or oil
  • Maple syrup

Description

French toast work with thick slices of French or Italian loaf bread that are several days old. That way they've had a chance to firm up, that make the slices hold up better when you dip them in the egg milk mixture and fry them. Thin slices of fresh bread tend to fall apart or get mushy when you do this, making for lousy French toast.

Steps

  1. Step 1: Whisk eggs, milk, and cinnamon.
  2. Step 2: Soak bread in mixture.
  3. Step 3: Fry until golden.
  4. Step 4: Serve warm.
